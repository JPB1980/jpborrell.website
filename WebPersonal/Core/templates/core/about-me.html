Juan Pablo Borrell


Personal Life

Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina; August 30, 1980. I did all my studies in Buenos Aires. Once I finished high school, I looked for my independence and started working. In parallel; I also studied. I am married and have two children. I very much enjoy spending time outside with them and my wife playing, running, or having a barbecue. I currently live in Germany since July 2020.


Hobbies and free time

I like sports such as handball or riding a bicycle, but my passion is soccer. I try as much as possible to show my children that sport is very good, not only because it helps their health; but also as a way to socialize and make new friends. I also really like to read, especially history books or thrillers. I like travel and to know new places, customs and foods. Together with my wife, we like to share series and movies.


Career

I love technology and that is why I have been working in IT since I finished high school. I have more than 20 years of experience and throughout my career, I have worked in small companies and multinationals such as Ernst & Young (EY), Bayer AG or Accenture. In all this time I have been in different teams, developing different tasks and above all learning many different technologies. I also have essential courses and certifications to improve my knowledge. I like, as far as possible, to be as up-to-date as possible in terms of new technologies and always basing myself on my technological preferences.
